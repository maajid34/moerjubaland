About Us

Ministry of Energy & Water Resources – Jubaland

Established in 2014, the Ministry leads the Energy and Water sectors — shaping policy, expanding access, and stewarding resources for equitable development across Jubaland.

  • Energy & Water Policy
  • Access & Equity
  • Sustainable Infrastructure
  • Community Resilience

Our Vision

To provide safe, affordable water and energy for all — driving socio-economic progress and climate-resilient livelihoods.

Our Mission

To develop, manage, and equitably distribute water and energy resources through sustainable policies, strong institutions, and inclusive partnerships.
